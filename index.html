About CountYourName

CountYourName is a modern web application that helps you discover approximately how many people around the world share your name. Using statistical algorithms and global population data, we provide fascinating insights into name popularity.

How to Use This Website

  1. Enter Your Name: Type your first name into the search box on the homepage. The tool works best with single first names rather than full names.
  2. Click Search: Press the "Search" button or simply hit the Enter key on your keyboard to begin the analysis process.
  3. Wait for Analysis: Our system will search through global name databases and calculate statistics. This takes just a few seconds.
  4. View Your Results: You'll see a detailed breakdown including:
    • Total estimated people with your name worldwide
    • Popularity rank (Top 100 to 100K+)
    • Rarity score (1-5 stars)
    • Percentage of world population
    • Category (Very Common to Rare)
  5. Try More Names: Search for names of family members, friends, or historical figures to compare popularity!
